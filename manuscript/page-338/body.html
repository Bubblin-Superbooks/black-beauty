Give up the drink or break Polly's heart!' But thanks be to God, and my dear wife, my chains were broken, and now for ten years I have not tasted a drop, and never wish for it.”

“I've a great mind to try at it,” said Grant, “for 'tis a poor thing not to be one's own master.”

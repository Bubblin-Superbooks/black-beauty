“Six weeks!” said James; “why, it will be six months before he can be of much use! It will make you a deal of work, John.”

“Well,” said John with a laugh, “work and I are very good friends; I never was afraid of work yet.”

“You are a very good man,” said James. “I wish I may ever be like you.”

“I don't often speak of myself,” said John, “but as you are going away from us out into the world to shift for yourself I'll just tell you how I look on these things. I was just as old as Joseph when my father and mother died of the fever within ten days of each other, and left me and my cripple sister Nelly alone in the world, without a relation that we could look to for help.
